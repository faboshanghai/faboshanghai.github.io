Class 3

Today we learned how to use the design software Rhino in 3-dimensional mode and operate the 3D printer to make an object. Firstly, I made a standardized model with walls of different width, bridging and leaning surfaces. After the test, I started to design a model of a certain architecture---an egg-shape ellipsoid surrounded by many wave-shape walls. The model tend to use the transparentness of parallel walls and combination of glass-like material with stone-like material to indicate the nobleness and smoothness of water.
