Operation Pipeline


raster engraving vs vector scoring

raster engraving: Any shape filled in black will be engraved (text, shapes, bitmaps). The disposition of the parts that will be engraved is important in order to reduce cutting time (when engraving, laser swings left to right and moves bottom to top)

vector scoring: It is like cutting, but the laser is set to a lower power and faster speed.
