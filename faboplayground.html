FABO PlayGround
中国“数制”乐园

同济大学设计创意学院FablabO|“数制工坊”,Bio Builder，CDI，人工智能等多个实验室在多年的创客教育的实践基础上，逐渐为大学，高中，初中小学提供的一个教学实践的实验室建构方案体系，该体系以FABO Playground|“数制”乐园为核心一系列“Project BasedLearning|基于项目“的主题性实验室模块，包含有“可持续生态|Sustainability”,”生物合成|BIO Build”，“人工智能|ArtificialIntelligence”,“智能可穿戴|Wearable”，“机器人|Robotic”，“增强现实|AR+VR”，“3D打印|3D Printing”,”未来生活|Future Living“，“飞行器|Flying Objects”,等模块。每个模块方案包含“空间营建”，“硬件配置”和“课程设置”等三大功能，通过网络系统，形成互联网教学的新的模式和基础.
Labs such as Fablab O at College of Design and Innovation, Bio Builder，CDI, AI, are gradually help building a lab structure for colleges, high schools, and primary schools. A lab structure that based on many years’ practical experience of running Fablab. Set FABO playground as the core, the system contains a set of ‘project based learning’ lab models, including ‘Sustainability’, ‘BIO Build’, ’Artificial Intelligence’, ’Wearable’, ’Robotic’, ’AR+VR’, ’3D Printing’, ’Future Living’, ’Flying Objects’, etc. Each model contains three functions - ‘space building’, ‘hardware configuration’, and ‘curriculum designing’. Through internet system, it forms the new model and foundation of internet education.
