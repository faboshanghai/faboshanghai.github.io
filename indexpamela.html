what is the fabacademy?

It is a distributed educational model that lasts 6 months with hands-on learning experience.

The program covers:

    _project management
    _computer-aided design
    _computer-controlled cutting
    _electronics production
    _computer-controlled machining
    _electronics design
    _molding & casting
    _composites
    _embedded programming
    _3d scanning & printing
    _input devices
    _interface and application programming
    _mechanical design
    _output devices
    _networking and communications
    _machine design
    _applications and implications
    _project development
    _invention, intellectual property, and income

Every week we have a different project, and the final project has to integrate the above skills. So I will be documenting my progress and projects throughout the course.
